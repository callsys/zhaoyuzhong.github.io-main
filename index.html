Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate of LAMP (Learning And Machine Perception) in the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences , advised by Prof. Fang Wan and Prof. Qixiang Ye . I got a B.E. degree in Peking University, Beijing in June 2020.

My research interests include computer vision and deep learning, specifically for text detection and recognition, weakly supervised sensing, and multimodal large language models.
